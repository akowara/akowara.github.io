About CIBC

CIBC is one of Canada's largest banks, with a diverse range of financial services and products, making it a great place to develop a career in the financial and technical industries. I am responsible for evaluating and measuring the performance, speed, and stability of CIBC online banking and their native applications under different workloads. As a performance tester, I employ various frameworks, including Python-based Robot and C-based Virtual User Generator. The Virtual User Generator scripts are executed using LoadRunner, a performance testing tool that assesses the performance, speed, scalability, and reliability of applications. The tests aim to gather metrics such as response time, memory usage, and CPU usage, which are then analyzed to ensure they meet business requirements. If necessary, I will request fixes to optimize performance.
